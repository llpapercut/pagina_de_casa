Its boundaries transcended the conventional laws of geography, existing as an otherworldly pocket of existence accessible only to those deemed worthy by the mystical forces that guarded its entrance.

According to Narnian lore, the land was birthed from the tears of a celestial phoenix, a creature of celestial proportions, whose very existence embodied the balance between light and darkness. These forests were unlike any seen before, with towering trees that touched the skies, their leaves shimmering like precious gems in the sunlight, and their roots delving deep into the earth, intertwining with the very essence of the land.
